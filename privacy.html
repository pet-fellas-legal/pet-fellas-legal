Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-21

This Privacy Policy explains how Pet Fellas (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Photos you choose to upload for pet mood / photo analysis features.
  • Account information (such as a user ID; and if you sign in, your email address).
  • Approximate location (optional) if you grant permission for nearby / matching features.
  • Device & usage data (app events, crash logs, performance data) to improve reliability.
  • Advertising data (such as device identifiers) if ads are shown in the app.
  • Purchase/subscription status (premium/active subscription) to unlock paid features.

How We Use Information

  • To provide photo analysis and matching features.
  • To operate and improve the app (performance, bug fixing, fraud prevention).
  • To show ads and measure ad performance (where applicable).
  • To provide customer support and respond to requests.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore/Database, Storage, Cloud Functions) for backend features.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display banner/rewarded ads (if enabled in the app).
  • Apple for in-app purchases and subscription processing.

These providers may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Advertising

If the app displays ads, ad providers may use device identifiers and similar signals to serve and measure ads. You can control personalized ads via your device settings.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion as described below.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can deny location permission; nearby features may be limited.
  • You can stop using the app and uninstall it at any time.
  • You can request data deletion by contacting us.

Children
