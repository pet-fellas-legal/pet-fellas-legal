Privacy Policy

App: Pet Fellas

Last updated: 2025-12-21

Overview

Pet Fellas lets users analyze pet photos and use a pet matching feature. This policy explains what data we may process and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Account information (e.g., login identifiers if you sign in).
  • Pet photos that you choose to upload for analysis.
  • Usage data (basic app interactions and diagnostics).
  • Approximate location (only if you grant location permission for nearby features).

How We Use Data

  • To provide photo analysis and app features.
  • To improve reliability and performance.
  • To enable nearby matching features when permission is granted.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for analytics, ads, and backend infrastructure (for example, Firebase and Google Mobile Ads). These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Advertising

The app may show ads. Ad providers may use device identifiers or similar signals to serve and measure ads, subject to your device settings and applicable laws.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can deny location permission; nearby features may be limited.
  • You can stop using the app and uninstall it at any time.
